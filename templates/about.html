About ZeroTrace: Track Your Carbon Footprint

Project Overview

The Carbon Emission Dashboard helps individuals and organizations calculate, track, and reduce their carbon footprint. By entering daily consumption data, users can calculate emissions from electricity, petrol, diesel, wastewater, and natural gas. This helps raise awareness about the environmental impact of everyday activities and encourages sustainable living.

Features

  • Carbon Emission Calculators for various sources
  • Comparison of calculated emissions with global sustainability standards
  • Graphical representation of historical emissions data

Technologies Used

  • Backend: Python, Flask
  • Database: MySQL
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Visualization: Plotly

Our Vision

Our goal is to provide individuals and organizations with the tools and knowledge to better understand and reduce their carbon emissions, contributing to a healthier planet.

How to Use the Dashboard

1. Input your daily consumption data in the calculator.

2. View your total carbon emissions and compare them with global standards.

3. Check out historical emissions trends through interactive graphs.
